Brief overview of the destination

  1. Uluwatu Temple: A Balinese Hindu temple perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean, famous for its stunning sunsets and traditional Kecak fire dance performances.
  2. Tanah Lot Temple: A picturesque sea temple situated on a rock formation, known for its breathtaking sunsets and serene surroundings.
  3. Mount Batur: An active volcano offering breathtaking sunrise views and trekking opportunities.
  4. Ubud Monkey Forest: A nature reserve and Hindu temple complex home to over 700 Balinese long-tailed macaques.
  5. Seminyak Beach: A popular beach destination known for its luxurious resorts, fine dining, and vibrant nightlife.
  • Nasi Goreng: A traditional Indonesian fried rice dish, often served with chicken, seafood, or vegetables.
  • Martabak: A stuffed pancake with savory fillings such as eggs, vegetables, and meat.
  • Sate: Grilled meat skewers, typically made with chicken, beef, or pork, and served with spicy peanut sauce.
  • Gado-Gado: A refreshing salad of mixed vegetables, tofu, and tempeh, topped with peanut sauce.
  • Es Campur: A traditional Balinese dessert consisting of shaved ice, coconut milk, and various fruits.
